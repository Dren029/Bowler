Project Goal

Build a 5-level platform puzzle runner with detailed pixel sprites, enemy AI, a boss battle, and a short gameplay trailer. The game should be playable in a browser through an HTML5 export and be stable enough for classmates to finish.

Success Criteria

  1. All 5 levels are complete and playable from start to finish without crashing.
  2. The player can jump, solve at least 2 puzzle types per level, and take damage from enemies.
  3. Level 5 includes a boss battle as the final encounter.
  4. The game exports and runs in a browser via itch.io without errors.
  5. A 30-second trailer captures gameplay clearly and can be shared.

What Is Already True

Scene experience: done
GDScript: comfortable
Node system: understood
Sprites and animation: done

The only major unknown is HTML5 export, which is part of the research checklist.

Research Checklist

  • Godot Beginners Tutorial Series: scenes and nodes
  • Official docs: nodes and scene system
  • Importing and animating sprites in Godot
  • Official docs: HTML5 export guide

Hard Scope Cuts

These are binding and should not be added back during development.

  • No mobile or phone optimization.
  • No 3D graphics.
  • No online multiplayer.
  • No procedural level generation.
  • No voice acting.
  • No custom shaders or advanced graphics effects.

Stretch Goals

Only attempt these if all 5 levels and the boss are complete by the end of Week 5.

  • Custom sound effects and background music.
  • Enhanced enemy variety with 5 or more unique types.
  • Unlockable difficulty modes.
  • In-game tutorials and help screens.
  • Polished particle effects for attacks and events.
